INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Start the Game: Click the "START GAME" button to begin a new Tic Tac Toe game.
  2. Make a Move: Players take turns clicking on an empty cell to place their mark ("X" or "O").
  3. Winning Condition: The first player to align three of their marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins the game.
  4. Avoid Draw: If the entire board is filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a draw.
  5. Scorekeeping: The game keeps track of the score, indicating the number of wins for each player.
  6. Challenge Opponent: Play against a friend or challenge yourself against the computer's AI in single-player mode.
